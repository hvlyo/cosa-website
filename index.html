About CoSA

Founded in 1999, the Ateneo Collegiate Society of Advertising aspires to bring individuals from all backgrounds closer to the field of advertising by teaching and challenging them to communicate ideas creatively and strategically.

Our Vision

To bring individuals from all backgrounds closer to the field of advertising, through teaching and challenging them to communicate ideas creatively and strategically.

Our Mission

Train Professionals

To train aspiring creative professionals to become competent individuals in the advertising industry by honing their skills in account management, project management, strategic conceptualization, and creative execution.

Expose Youth

To expose the youth and the Ateneo community to the advertising industry by providing them with opportunities to engage in the field of advertising and other creative fields.

Inspire Action

To ideate and execute innovative advertisements and campaigns that inspire real-world action.

What We Do

Campaigns

Real-world advertising campaigns for local businesses and organizations.

Trainings

Professional development workshops and skill-building sessions.

Events

Networking events, industry talks, and creative showcases.

Youth Exposure

Mentoring programs and outreach to inspire future advertisers.
